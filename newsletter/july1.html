A Blessing for the Week

“Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding;
in all your ways submit to him, and he will make your paths straight.”

— Proverbs 3:5

As we begin July, let us lean our hearts, thoughts, dreams, and lives on Jesus — trusting Him to provide the perfect way.

As we celebrate America’s 250th year and enjoy the summer season, we want to thank you for taking the time to serve with such love, kindness, and friendship in our children’s ministry. Together, let us create wonderful summer memories and share the kindness of Jesus with the children and families who walk through the doors of our community this Sunday.

As this week begins, may we be ready to love as God loves us and help make this summer remarkable for every child. Here are some important details for this week ahead.
